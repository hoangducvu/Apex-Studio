Processing

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Frames with a custom lens colour may need an extra 2–3 days before dispatch. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking as soon as your order ships.

Orders shipped within the EU incur no additional customs charges. For non-EU destinations, any import duties or local taxes are the responsibility of the customer.
